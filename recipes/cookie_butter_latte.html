Cookie Butter Latte Recipe


Cookie flavored latte? Yes.

Cookie flavored latte in 10 minutes? YES.

My Recipe? No.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cookie butter
  • 1/2 cup hot coffee
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup hot milk

How to make

  1. Whisk cookie butter and hot coffee until dissolved.
  2. Add and stir in brown sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Froth hot milk and pour frothed milk over coffee, pour foam over frothed milk.
  4. Drink :)
